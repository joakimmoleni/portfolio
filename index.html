    About

    I spent over a decade in operational leadership before moving into software engineering. That background shapes how I think about systems: ownership, reliability and what it takes to keep things running when it counts.

    Today I build and maintain transaction-critical software in core banking, where mainframe logic and modern services have to work together without excuses.

    • Web

      Comfortable across the modern web stack — from REST APIs and Node.js services to frontend interfaces. I prefer solutions that stay readable and do what they need to without unnecessary layers.

    • Integrations

      Most of my day-to-day work involves bridging mainframe transaction flows with distributed service layers. Getting data across those boundaries reliably is harder than it sounds — and where I add the most value.

    • Infrastructure

      Experience spanning COBOL, HLASM, Node.js, Python and Linux environments. I care most about the parts that hold everything together: service design, deployment pipelines and runtime decisions that outlast the sprint.

    • Data & Databases

      Worked with SQL, IMS and transactional data in regulated banking environments. I focus on how data flows between systems, where consistency breaks down and how to prevent it in high-volume production.
